Training in BJJ

My favorite hobby is Brazillian Jiu Jitsu: I have been training since I was 14 years old.
If you don't know what BJJ is I will explain briefly:
BJJ or "Brazillian Jiu-Jitsu" is a form of martial arts that doesn't utilize stikes, but specializes in grappling, takedowns, ground-fighting, and submissions. BJJ is a great form of exercise, one of the most respected forms of self-defense, and is also a great way to keep you humble!
